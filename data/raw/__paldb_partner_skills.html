Partner Skills

These are unique abilities that each Pal species possesses.
Some Pals have Partner Skills that allow them to shoot guns or to have the player ride them.

Some Pals require |Pal Gear|, which can be made at the Pal Gear Workbench, to unlock their Partner Skills.

These skills can be checked from the Party tab. Some Pals provide a benefit just by being in your Party.
